# Welcome Recruiters — Video Script

Two ~45-55 second scripts for the `/welcome-recruiters` landing page pitch — one to record in English, one in Hebrew. They carry the same points but aren't a literal translation of each other, so each sounds natural spoken aloud rather than read.

**Recording notes**

- Look at the camera lens, not the screen — that's what reads as eye contact.
- Read each script through a couple of times first, then talk through it loosely rather than reciting word-for-word. A little imperfection sounds more human than a stiff recitation.
- Natural pauses at the line breaks below double as breathing points.
- Once recorded, save the files as `public/videos/welcome-recruiters-en.mp4` and `public/videos/welcome-recruiters-he.mp4` — the site already points at those paths and switches between them with the language toggle.

## English

Hi, I'm Gal Ben-Abu — thanks for taking a minute to watch this.

I recently finished my service in the IDF, and alongside that I trained as a developer: cyber fundamentals through Magshimim, then a full-stack bootcamp at HackerU, where I worked with React, Node, and MongoDB.

Right now I'm prepping for the psychometric exam ahead of a Software Engineering degree, and I'm actively looking for my first role as a junior developer — a team where I can keep learning fast, take ownership, and build things that matter.

If that sounds like someone you'd want on your team, my full portfolio is one click below — projects, code, and more about how I work.

Thanks again for watching, and I hope we get to talk soon.

## עברית

היי, אני גל בן אבו — תודה שהקדשת רגע לצפות בסרטון הזה.

לפני זמן קצר סיימתי את השירות שלי בצה"ל, ובמקביל התמחתי בפיתוח תוכנה: יסודות סייבר במגשימים, ואז קורס פול סטאק ב-HackerU, שם עבדתי עם React, Node ו-MongoDB.

כרגע אני מתכונן לפסיכומטרי לקראת תואר בהנדסת תוכנה, ובמקביל מחפש את התפקיד הראשון שלי כמפתח ג'וניור — צוות שבו אני יכול ללמוד מהר, לקחת אחריות, ולבנות דברים שבאמת משנים.

תודה שוב על הצפייה, ומקווה שנדבר בקרוב.
