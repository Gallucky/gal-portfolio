About Me

I served in the IDF in a cybersecurity-adjacent role, where I first got hands-on with systems thinking, structured problem-solving, and working under real operational pressure — habits that carry directly into how I approach software today.

After my service, I joined Magshimim, an intensive cyber training program, where I sharpened my technical foundations and learned how to break down complex systems methodically rather than guessing my way through them.

From there I moved into a hands-on full-stack bootcamp, building real projects end to end with React, Node.js, and MongoDB. I'm now actively job-hunting for my first developer role, and looking forward to turning that training into fast, accessible, production-ready web apps for a real team.
